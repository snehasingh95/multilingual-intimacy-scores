This folder consists of the monolingual model.
Our script is train_intimacy_model.py for training and testing the model. The configurations are in config.py.

The pre-requisites are mentioned in prerequisites.txt.
The arguments to train and test our model are specified in train_test_args.txt.

We have created three models, one for each language english, french and chinese.
